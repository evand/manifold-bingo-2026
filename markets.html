Eligibility Criteria

Markets must meet all of these criteria to be eligible for bingo cards:

  • Binary outcome - YES/NO resolution only
  • Listed - Not unlisted/private
  • Ranked - Passes Manifold's quality filter
  • Minimum 20 unique traders
  • Close date - Between 1 week from card creation and Jan 7, 2027

Markets are also excluded if their title suggests non-binary resolution (lottery markets, conditional markets, "resolves to probability", etc.) or if they're manually excluded for quality reasons.
